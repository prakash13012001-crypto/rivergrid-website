Our Services

Specialist water resource engineering services that reduce risk, meet compliance, and deliver lasting value.

🌧️

Hydrological Studies

Accurate flood estimation and rainfall analysis to support safe infrastructure design, regulatory approvals, and disaster risk reduction. We use proven methodologies to deliver defensible results.

🌊

Hydraulic Modelling

1D/2D hydraulic models (HEC-RAS, MIKE) to predict flood behaviour, quantify risk, and inform design decisions for bridges, culverts, retention structures, and drainage systems.

🗺️

GIS & Remote Sensing

Spatial data analysis and high-quality mapping to visualise catchments, flood extents, and infrastructure corridors — enabling smarter planning and faster stakeholder communication.

💧

Water Supply Design

End-to-end design of water supply schemes — from source assessment and demand forecasting to distribution network design — ensuring reliable, safe, and cost-efficient supply.

♻️

Wastewater Management

Sustainable wastewater treatment and drainage solutions designed to protect public health, meet pollution control norms, and pass regulatory inspection at first submission.

📋

DPR Preparation

Comprehensive Detailed Project Reports prepared to government and funding agency standards, ensuring faster approvals, accurate cost estimates, and successful project sanctioning.
